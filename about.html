About Me

My name is Luke Vaughan. I'm an analytical thinker and problem solver. I created this website to showcase my work.

I graduated from College of Charleston in May 2022 with a master's degree in mathematical sciences. I have a passion for applying mathematical/statistical/computational techniques to solve problems in ecology and environmental science. I'm particularly interested in spatial ecology, pattern formation, carbon dynamics, biodiversity, and ecological disturbances.

I also have a passion for exposition. I have designed several presentations to explain plots, figures, and results from my projects. I can present to an audience of non-experts, by putting my explanations in layman's terms, or to a panel of experts, such as a thesis committee.

My work and personal life both reflect a love of nature. I enjoy diving into outdoor activities in my free time. Some of my hobbies include hiking, biking, camping, wakesurfing, and snowboarding.
